Spicy Calamari

A plate of calamari fried in light batter along with banana peppers.

This is a traditional Greek dish served with Tzatziki Sauce.

Ingredients

  • Squid rings
  • Banana peppers
  • Batter
  • Frying oil

Instructions

  1. Coat squid rings and banana peppers in batter mix
  2. Set fryer to 80 degrees and drop squid rings and banana peppers into fryer
  3. Once crispy, plate and serve along with tzatziki sauce
