Adobo

Adobo is a traditional filipino stew made with slow cooked pork or chicken, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Potatoes
  • Pepper corns
  • Pork broth

Instructions

  1. Lather pork in pepper corns
  2. Dice potatoes
  3. Bring pot of water and pork broth to a slow simmer
  4. Mix pork and potatoes in pot and leave for 2 hours
  5. Once pork is tender, plate and serve
